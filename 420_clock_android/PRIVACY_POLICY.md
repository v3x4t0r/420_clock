Welcome to **420 Clock** ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the 420 Clock mobile application (the "App").

## 2. Information We Collect
**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.** 

The 420 Clock app is designed to function entirely offline and locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect:

* Location data (GPS).
* Usage analytics or crash reports.

## 3. How the App Works
The App calculates time zones locally on your device using standard time libraries. It does not transmit your local time, timezone, or any other data to external servers. 

## 4. Third-Party Services

## 5. Children's Privacy
